Fill the Buckets by Justin Strauss

How to play: Click above the line to release drops of water. Every time a drop lands in a moving bucket, you earn a point. If the drop misses all buckets and falls off the bottom of the screen, you lose a point.
The game is over once one of the buckets overflows by being hit by a drop of water when it has already turned blue. Keep in mind that the highest attainable score is 10.
