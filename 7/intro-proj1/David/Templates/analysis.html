My Analysis of the Data

Biggest Waste Producing Districts

Interestingly, there was not a single district with the most refuse, paper, and metal/glass/plastics tons collected. Community district 12 in Queens was the highest in refuse tons collected, up at 7201.87 tons. Community district 7 in Manhattan was the highest in paper tons collected, up at 1232.56 tons. And finally, District 8 of Manhattan was the highest in metal/glass/plastic tons collected, with 643.79 tons collected. It is not surprising that two of these districts are in Manhattan, as it is a very densely populated and compact island with a high capability in making trash.
