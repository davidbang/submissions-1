BALL DODGER

Instructions

Once you enter the canvas, you are the pointer.
Your score is determined by how many balls are on the screen.
You may say, "But Matthew, I don't see any balls on the screen"
. At the moment, your sissy self may be right. But click once, and you will add a ball to the screen.
As you go, you must move your mouse to survive the onslaught of balls, and simultaneously keep adding more to increase your score.
This is the paradox of
